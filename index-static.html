About Us

Your home deserves care you can trust. We work for homeowners, property owners and real estate professionals alike. That’s how we’ve worked from day one.

M

Mission

To make your house feel like home again: one team for painting, cleaning, repairs and curb appeal. Whether you're selling, moving in or refreshing your space, we're here so your home looks and feels its best.

V

Vision

To be Ottawa’s go-to choice for property preparation: reliable, on time and with standards that show in every room we touch.

V

Values

  • Trust: no deposit, even on large projects. You pay when the work is done.
  • Family and team: over 15 years working together, same standards every time.
  • Quality: we grow by referrals and repeat clients, not just ads.
  • Respect for your home: we treat your space like our own.

In six years we have never asked for a deposit. Our clients pay when the work is complete and delivered as agreed.
